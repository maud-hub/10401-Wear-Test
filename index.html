10401 advantages vs. 10400

  • Sweetheart neckline design: 4.46/5 and 81% positive, no equivalent on 10400.
  • Anti-slip silicone strip: 4.06/5 and 81% rated 4-5, a new feature.
  • Underband stays in place: 88% vs. 54% on 10400 (+34pp).
  • Cup fit/no-uniboob: 75% just right vs. 10400, where 40% complained of squishing.

Watch-outs before bulk

  • Comfort: 3.47 all / 3.68 excl. DDD+ vs. 4.3 on 10400. Strapless is inherently harder than bandeau with straps.
  • Fit: 3.73 all / 3.94 excl. DDD+ vs. 4.2. L and XL DDD+ cup wearers sizing by band are driving the gap.
  • Support: 42% say not enough (all) / 35% excl. DDD+, concentrated in L (60%) and XL (50%).
  • True to size: 65% all / 70% excl. DDD+. Size chart review recommended for L/XL.
